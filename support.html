Our Return Policy

We want you to love your ItsShotime gear! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return unworn, unwashed items within 30 days of delivery.

How to Return

  • Contact us through our contact form with your order number
  • We'll send you return instructions and a prepaid shipping label
  • Pack the item securely in original packaging (if possible)
  • Ship it back using the provided label
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the return

Need a different size? We're happy to help! Contact us and we'll guide you through the exchange process. Exchanges are free - we'll cover the shipping!

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items worn or washed
  • Items without original tags
  • Sale or clearance items (final sale)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are your t-shirts made from?
All our graphic tees are made from 100% premium cotton for maximum comfort and durability. The fabric is pre-shrunk and designed to maintain its shape and color after multiple washes.
How do I care for my ItsShotime t-shirt?
For best results: Machine wash cold with like colors, tumble dry low, and avoid bleach. Turn inside out before washing to protect the print. Do not iron directly on the design.
What if my size is out of stock?
We restock popular items regularly! Contact us and we can notify you when your size is back in stock. You can also check back on our shop page - we update inventory frequently.
Do you offer gift cards?
Not yet, but this feature is coming soon! For now, if you'd like to purchase a gift, we recommend choosing a size based on our size guide below.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
We process orders quickly! If you need to cancel or modify your order, contact us immediately. We'll do our best to help, but once an order has shipped, we can only process returns or exchanges.
Are your designs screen printed or heat transfer?
All our designs are high-quality screen prints, not heat transfers. This ensures vibrant colors that last and won't peel or crack over time.
Do you have a physical store?
We're currently online only, which allows us to keep our prices low and reach customers everywhere. Follow us on social media for pop-up shop announcements!
